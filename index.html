About Us

The Cats & Dogs Foundation

Our foundation was founded in 2017 and our goal is to help find a home and family for each foster animal possible. We are a non-profit orginazation and our service is free of charge to anyone looking to adopt a new family member. Feel free to look through a selection of animals in your area hosted on our site, if you need assistance in anything you may contact us at Cats&Dogs@gmail.com
